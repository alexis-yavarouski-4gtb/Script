News

Hard Bass School is a duo of hardbass producers from St. Petersburg, becoming extremely popular and created soundtracks to the movies "Okolofutbola" and "Black Water", throwing Pumping Hard Bass parties internationally and is an unstoppable hardbass powerhouse signed to Hard Bass Crew, they popularized the record label with hit album Hard Bass School: The Album, the most successful hardbass album.
